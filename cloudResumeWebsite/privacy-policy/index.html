Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 15, 2025

Summary
  • We do not collect or share personal data.
  • The app only accesses PDF files you explicitly choose to open.

1. Overview

PDF Viewer (the “App”) respects your privacy. This policy explains what information — if any — the App collects and how we handle files you open. By using the App you agree to the terms described here.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information, analytics, usage data, device identifiers, location, or other user content. We do not track you or sell your data.

3. File Access

The App only accesses PDF files that you explicitly choose to open via the file picker or by selecting a file from within the app. The App does not scan, index, or enumerate the files on your device.

All rendering and processing of PDF files occurs locally on your device. Files are not uploaded to any external server by the App.

4. Third-Party Components

The App uses the open-source AndroidPdfViewer library to render PDF documents. That library operates locally and does not transmit your files or personal data to us or to the library authors. AndroidPdfViewer is licensed under the Apache License 2.0 .

5. Internet and Cloud Files

The App does not require an internet connection for core functionality. If you open a PDF from a cloud provider (for example Google Drive or Dropbox), fetching that file is handled by your OS or provider — not by the App. Those files may be subject to the cloud provider’s privacy terms.

6. Data Sharing

Because the App does not collect or store user data, we do not share or disclose personal information to third parties.

7. Security

Rendering is done locally to reduce exposure. You should still exercise caution when opening PDFs from unknown sources; a malicious PDF file could harm your device.

8. Children

The App does not knowingly collect information from children. If you are under the age required by local law to consent to app use, please use the App only with parental supervision.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. We will post the updated version at this same URL with a revised “Last Updated” date.
